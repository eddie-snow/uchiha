Uchiha Clan

The Uchiha Clan (うちは一族, Uchiha Ichizoku) is one of the four noble clans of Konoha, reputed to be the village's strongest because of their Sharingan and natural battle prowess
After helping found Konoha decades ago, the Uchiha grew increasingly isolated from the village's affairs, culminating in most of their deaths during the Uchiha Clan Downfall
Few Uchiha now survive into the present day.
